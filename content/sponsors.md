Our meetups are free to attend, and that's only possible because of sponsors
who cover food, refreshments, and the occasional venue.

## Why sponsor?

- Get your name (and people) in front of a room full of the folks who
  evaluate, buy, and operate Apple management tooling in NYC.
- Support the community your customers live in.
- Sponsors are thanked on the event page, in the RSS announcement, and at
  the meetup itself.

## What sponsorship looks like

A typical meetup sponsorship covers food and refreshments for the group.
Sponsors are welcome to attend, say a brief hello, and bring stickers — we
keep hard sales pitches out of the program per our
[code of conduct](../code-of-conduct/).

## Interested?

Email us at [info@nycmacadmins.com](mailto:info@nycmacadmins.com) and we'll
find you a month.

## Thank you

Thanks to every sponsor who has fed this community — you make the meetups
happen. Recent sponsors are credited on their [event pages](../events/).
