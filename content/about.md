NYC Mac Admins is a community meetup for the people who manage Apple devices
for a living — Mac admins, client platform engineers, help desk teams, security
folks, and IT leaders in and around New York City.

We get together regularly for presentations, open discussion, and food. Topics
range from MDM and deployment to security, automation, identity, and whatever
else the community is wrestling with that month.

## Who should come?

Anyone who touches Apple devices at work. Whether you manage five Macs or five
thousand, whether you're deep into `zsh` or just got handed "the Apple stuff" —
you're welcome here.

## How it works

- Meetups are **free** — we just ask that you register so we know how much food to order.
- Each meetup usually features a presentation from a community member or vendor engineer, followed by open discussion.
- Sponsors cover food and refreshments. Interested in sponsoring? Email us at [info@nycmacadmins.com](mailto:info@nycmacadmins.com).

## Stay in the loop

- Join the [Mac Admins Slack](https://www.macadmins.org/) and hop into [#newyork](https://macadmins.slack.com/archives/C3APLCT1R) — announcements land there first.
- Subscribe to our [events RSS feed](../events/index.xml).
- Email us anytime: [info@nycmacadmins.com](mailto:info@nycmacadmins.com).
