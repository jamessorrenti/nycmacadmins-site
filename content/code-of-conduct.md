description: "NYC Mac Admins code of conduct — how we treat each other at meetups and online."
---

NYC Mac Admins is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for
everyone. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form, at
meetups or in our online spaces.

## The short version

Be kind. Be professional. Treat everyone with respect, regardless of role,
employer, experience level, gender, gender identity and expression, sexual
orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, ethnicity, age, religion,
or nationality.

## The longer version

- **Harassment** includes offensive comments, deliberate intimidation,
  stalking, unwelcome photography or recording, sustained disruption of talks,
  inappropriate physical contact, and unwelcome sexual attention.
- Participants asked to stop any harassing behavior are expected to comply
  immediately.
- Sponsors and speakers are also subject to this policy.
- **Vendor pitches**: sponsors and vendors are part of our community, but
  meetups are not sales floors. Keep it technical and useful.

## Reporting

If you are being harassed, notice that someone else is being harassed, or have
any other concerns, contact the organizers at
[info@nycmacadmins.com](mailto:info@nycmacadmins.com) or find an organizer at
the meetup. Reports are handled confidentially.

Organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning the
offender or expulsion from the meetup and community spaces.

*Adapted from community conference anti-harassment policies used across the
Mac Admins community.*
